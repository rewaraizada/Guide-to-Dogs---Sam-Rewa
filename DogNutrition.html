DOG NUTRITION



One ingredient to keeping your dog healthy is good nutrition!


  • Most adult dogs will need a low-calorie diet. It should contain less than 350 calories per cup.

  • Your veterinarian will tell you how many calories your dog needs a day.

  • For adult dogs feeding 1 to 2 times a day is recommended.

  • For puppies 6-8 weeks old = 4-6 meals a day.

  • 6 months = 2-3 meals a day & they have reached 75% of their adult size.

  • It is important to keep the right food in the puppies younger ages because of growth being completed by 8 to 10 months of age.

  • After 12 months the food can change to what adult dogs would eat.

  • Make sure to consider the smaller vs. larger breed food depending on what dog type you have!
